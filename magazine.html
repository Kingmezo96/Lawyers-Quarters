Henry Barnabas Ehi, MICA

From the Editor's Desk introduces the second edition of Lawyers' Quarters Magazine as a special issue dedicated to vision, transformation and impact.

The note frames the edition around Dr. Prada Uzodimma's 30th birthday story, Mazi Afam Osigwe, SAN's leadership of the Nigerian Bar Association, and two major legal events: NBA-SBL's Business Law Conference and the NBA Annual General Conference.
